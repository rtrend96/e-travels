Agra 

The city of Taj Mahal a trifle cleaner
& greener if you have been here before.
Agra-the name itself recalls the picture
of Taj Mahal,a spectacular monument of 
love built of white marble.Agra city is 
not only a reputed tourist destination 
but also a shopper's paradise. It offers
breathtakingly beautiful handcrafted 
items that are made of pure leather.Agra
is also famous for the replicas of Taj 
Mahal. Furthermore,it also offers 
excursions to Fatehpur Sikri,Mathura and
Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary that make it a 
complete tourist destination.
