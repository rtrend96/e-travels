Golden Temple 

The Golden Temple is the
ultimate Sikh pilgrimage.
The Harmandir Sahib, as 
it is traditionally known,
actually means the temple
of Hari or the Supreme God.
Also known as the Darbar
Sahib, the stupendous,
architectural phenomena is
located at the city of
Amritsar.
  
  

	The temple's exterior is gold-plated and the structure
stands in the middle of a square tank. There is a causeway
across the Pool of Nectar to reach the Temple.The shrine is
symbolical of the doctrines of Sikhism. It also represents the
magnificent strength of all the Sikhs. The amazing thing about
Harmandir Sahib is that it has doorways on all four sides, 
meant to be open for the people of the all four castes. All
over the, the Sikhs always look forward to visit to the
magnificent temple and offer prayers at the Harmandir Sahib.
