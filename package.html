Adventure Trip To Ladakh
  If you are looking for an offbeat destination, there could be no better place than Ladakh. The magnificence of its barren mountains, rugged and arid landscapes and the contrasting appearance of rivers and natural lakes make Ladakh a veritable natural wonder. This holiday package takes you to centuries-old monasteries, palaces and the highest mountain passes in the world. Package Highlights
  • Shanti Stupa and Leh Palace
  • Alchi and Likir monasteries
  • Confluence of Indus and Zanskar rivers
  • Gurudwara Pathar Sahib and Magnetic Hill
  • Basgo Palace and Leh Hall of Fame
  • Hemis and Thiksey monasteries
  • Shey Palace and Druk Pema School (of the '3 Idiots' fame)
  • Samsthaling Monastery, Panamik village and Yarab Tso Lake
  • Chang La and Pangong Lake
