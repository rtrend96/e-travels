Panjim 

Panaji, the capital city of Goa is
the charming little travel hub for
the tourists coming to Goa. Though,
it does not possess several world
renowned monuments, its bears an 
ambience which has a captivating
charm. Panjim or Panaji is one of
the India's smallest & pleasantest
state capitals and is located on 
the south bank of the wide Mandovi
River. The town has preserved its 
Portuguese heritage appreciably well
and parts of it still contains 
narrow winding streets, old houses
with over hanging balconies, red-
tiled roofs and numerous small bars
and cafe's.
