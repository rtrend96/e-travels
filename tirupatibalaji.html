The town of Tirupati-Balaji is one
of the most ancient & sacred pil-
grimage spots in India. The name
Tirupati-Balaji means the 'Lord of
Lakshmi',& should have been applied
to the village on the Venkat hill,
the dwelling of Lord Venkateswara.
However,Tirupati-Balaji is the town
and transport hub at the bottom of
the hill. The hill near the temple
is called Tirumala or the'sacred
hill'.
  
  

One of the major attractions
in Tirupati-Balaji is the 
world famous Sri Venkatesh-
wara Temple. As a large no. 
of devotees visit this temple 
everyday, be prepared to wait
for almost 20 hours before
you can get the darshan of 
the deity.
